client: Reader's Digest
designer: Diana Levin

This is a premium designed for Reader’s Digest. I handled the look, page layout and photo manipulation of the publication. I built graphics, and chose stock photos and other forms of artwork to create the inside pages.

The cover was designed in four colors and the interior was designed in grey scale. All the subtitles in this premium are unique, and were treated as individual logos.
